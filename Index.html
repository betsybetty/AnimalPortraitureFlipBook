Eyes and Smiles

Watching faces as I go,

Animated smiles and sparkling eyes.

With paint and brush; always intentional.

To give a smile from oneself to another.

(Dr Betty Kibirige)

than a learned one.

(Thomas a Kempis)

longer strive for our own way; but
